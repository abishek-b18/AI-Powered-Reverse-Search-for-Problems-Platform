# 🚀 AI-Powered Reverse Search for Problems

## 📌 Overview
AI-Powered Reverse Search for Problems is an innovative platform designed to discover real-world problems before they become mainstream opportunities. Instead of generating random startup ideas, the platform continuously scans social media, product reviews, research papers, news articles, discussion forums, and industry reports to identify recurring complaints, unmet needs, research gaps, and emerging challenges.

## ❗ Problem Statement
Millions of people discuss frustrations, challenges, and unmet needs online every day. However, these valuable insights remain scattered across multiple platforms, making it difficult to identify which problems are worth solving.

### Challenges
- Students struggle to find meaningful project ideas.
- Entrepreneurs build products in saturated markets.
- Researchers spend significant time finding research gaps.
- Investors lack visibility into emerging opportunities.
- Organizations miss valuable customer pain points.

## 💡 Proposed Solution
The AI-Powered Reverse Search for Problems platform automatically discovers high-impact problems by analyzing public discussions, reviews, research publications, and online communities.

The system:
- Detects recurring complaints
- Identifies unmet needs
- Evaluates market demand
- Generates Opportunity Scores
- Suggests solution directions

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify real-world problems from multiple sources
- Detect recurring complaints and frustrations
- Rank opportunities using AI
- Help users discover startup opportunities
- Support researchers and innovators
- Create a centralized problem-discovery platform
